Frequently Asked Questions

What types of bags do you offer at CarryCo?

We offer a wide range of bags, including backpacks, handbags, tote bags, duffle bags, and more. Each bag is crafted for style, functionality, and durability to suit various lifestyles and preferences.

How can I place an order on CarryCo?

To place an order, browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You’ll be prompted to enter your shipping and payment details. Once your order is confirmed, we’ll email you the details along with an order tracking link.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard and more). All transactions are encrypted for safety.

How much is the shipping fee?

Shipping fees vary by location and weight of the order. Exact shipping costs will display at checkout. Free shipping is available for orders over a certain amount, as indicated on our homepage.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Orders typically take 2-5 business days to process. Once shipped, delivery times vary by location, generally 5-7 business days for domestic shipping and 10-15 days for international shipping.

Can I return or exchange a bag if I’m not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and accompanied by a receipt for a refund or exchange.

How do I care for my bag to make it last longer?

For fabric bags, spot-clean with mild soap and a damp cloth. For leather bags, use a leather cleaner and conditioner. Avoid excessive sunlight or moisture.

Do you have a warranty on your bags?

Yes, all CarryCo bags come with a 1-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects. This does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, or accidental damage.

How can I contact CarryCo customer support?

Reach our support team via email at support@carryco.com or through our website’s Contact Us page. We’re available Monday through Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM (GMT).

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

Yes, we often run promotions and discounts, which are posted on our site. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on exclusive offers and deals.
